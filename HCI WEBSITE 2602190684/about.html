Our History

Jockey's Conference Center (JCC) is a famous conference center located in Jakarta, Indonesia. Established in 1974, JCC has played a significant role in hosting numerous national and international conferences, exhibitions, fairs, indoor sports events, and musical concerts. Over the years, JCC has been the venue for prestigious events such as the 11th Non-Aligned Movement Conference and the Asian-African Conference. With its rich history and reputation, JCC has become a symbol of excellence in the event industry.
